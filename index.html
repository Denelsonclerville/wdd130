Marfranc

Marfranc is a town in the southeast Haiti. It is bordered by Moron to the east,Chanbelan to the northeast, Abricot to the north, Didon to the south, and Lassise to the west. It also touches a corner of Jeremie D.C in the southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation.
